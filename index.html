Alexandria Glass Recycling Locations and Population Density

Alexandria citizens created this website in support of a query to city leaders about potential improvements to glass recycling in the city we are proud to call home. It shows population density by census block group for the city of Alexandria and one mile rings around each of the city's five glass recycling drop offs. Much of the city is far from any drop off location.

We made it using American Community Survey data and free tools as documented in this code. We would be thrilled if citizens learned from our work to illustrate the effects of public programs and inform dialog in their own communities. We welcome questions and comments by e-mail.
